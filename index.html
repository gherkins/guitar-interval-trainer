Instructions

You will (hopefully) hear two notes being played.
The first one will be shown on the fretboard.

You will then have to guess the second note by clicking a correct string and fret on the fretboard.

You can narrow down the intervals being played by deselecting the checkboxes at the bottom
You can only guess once per notes played
You can replay the notes as often as you like by clicking on the "Replay notes"-button
